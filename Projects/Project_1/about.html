About

Who Are We?

We are a team of high experienced processional, and home chefs who have a deep Pasion for cooking and food. With a wide range of experience levels on our team we are able to cater to a wider audience of people and provide a more inclusive and comprehensive look into the world of cooking. Ww work with some of the top knife manufacturers in the industry to bring you current information about knife care and cooking practices.



Our Goal

Chefy aims to making cooking more enjoyable and allow for all experience levels to feel more comfortable in the kitchen.

What We Stand For

We believe that no matter your gender, age, race, or culture, everyone has a place in the kitchen. Food is in itself a language that can be universal in drawing people to the dinner table. Cooking should be a skill that everyone knows and should be at least some what skilled at. We welcome all backgrounds to the table and welcome you to learn more.
